Aaron Irizarry
Designnin a Culture of Design
Workplace culture doesn't start with beanbags, foosball tables, or a beer fridge, and it doesn't end with neckles, PCs, or big corporations. It's the unwritten rules, behavior beliefs, and the motivations that enable good work to get done, or it's what stifles a workforce. For design to be most effective and for designers to feel valued, we need to work in a culture that embraces design and allows it to succed.
